* Org Me Up!
** Where am I?

Hi, there. Welcome to Org-Me-Up. This is cliffnotes on org-roids. Learn about anything. Mostly nerdy anythings. Then, forget about everything, and refer back to your notes.

*** Installing Org-Me-Up

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
# step 1: clone me
git clone git@github.com:evannagle/org-me-up.git

# step 2: spend 14 years learning Emacs and Emacs org mode
open "https://orgmode.org/"

# step 3: come back when your jeans are silken, and pick a tome:
ls org-me-up/notes
emacs org-me-up/the-awk-programming-language/readme.org
#+END_SRC

*** How Do I Contribute

Interested in contributing? Why, you kind traveller! Here's how:

1. Fork this repo.
2. Add a PDF of the book to the [[file+emacs:books/][books/]] folder (e.g. books/practical-common-lisp).
3. Add your org-based book notes to the [[emacs%2Bfile:notes/][notes/]] folder. Take a peak at another set of notes to get an idea for how things work around here.
4. Link to your book in the /library/ section of this document.
5. Make sure to follow the hackneyed citation style, and tag your books for easy lookup.
6. Submit a pull request to get your book added to the main branch. Or, horde your learnings like a lemming.
